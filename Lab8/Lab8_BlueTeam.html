How does the least expensive state change throughout the years? 

mattdf <- df %>%group_by(Year) %>%
  summarize(Cheapest = min(Cost), Expensive = max(Cost))

ggplot(mattdf) +
  geom_line(aes(x=Year, y=Cheapest, group=1), color="blue") +
  geom_line(aes(x=Year, y=Expensive, group=1), color="orange") + 
  labs(y="Cost", title="Most expensive and least expensive cost per year") +
  scale_color_manual(values=c("Least Expensive", "Most Expensive"))

In the plot above, the yellow line represents the maximum cost per year. Similarly, the blue line represents the minimum cost per year. Clearly they both increase with time. However, the maximum increases at a faster rate than the minimum.
