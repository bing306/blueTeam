1. Do mothers who smoke have increased rates of premature delivery (before 270 days)?

This is an important question because premature delivery can lead to many health problems for the baby. Lung, heart, and brain problems, cerebral palsy, learning imparment, and chronic health issues are some known problems. (www.mayoclinic.org)
